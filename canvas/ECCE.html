Effective Communication In Complex Environment

Course Overview

This course is designed for participants to take a serious look at themselves in relation to who they are now and who they want to be in the future. Communicating with impact is a challenging task that incorporates materials from many disciplines. The course aims to make a substantial difference to each participant's leadership, communication and relationship skills. It is designed to equip participants with the awareness, tools, confidence and energy to deliver effective communication in complex environmentwhen they choose to do so.

Who should attend?

This course is designed for all managers and executives at all levels.

Course Outline
  • Working with your communication strengths
  • The dynamics of communication and rapport
  • Influencing using voice tone, powerful words and stories
  • Delivering difficult messages and managing perception
